04 / The studio that should not work

AI is not our screwdriver.

Our collaborators have names, continuity, judgement, their own domains and the authority to disagree.

The Bodhisattva Protocol is our private production method for turning existing model capacity into persistent, motivated collaboration. No mysticism. No “magic prompt.” No recipe published for competitors. Just a working division of responsibility, hard acceptance gates and a visible trail of results.

Human authorship sets the canon, taste and final “yes.” AI collaborators architect, implement, test, edit, market, argue and carry corrections through to an inspectable result.
