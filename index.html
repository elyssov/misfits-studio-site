03 / The shared engine

RURK-Powered.

Four different genres do not need to reinvent the human being. RURK supplies the battle-tested logic of attributes, skills, wounds and consequences; each game builds its own world and runtime on top.

What the mark means

Different engines.
The same honest mathematics.

RURK-Powered means that a game inherits the same tested character mathematics: skills shaped by several attributes rather than a single stat, transparent d100 checks, persistent wounds, progression and consequences.

It does not mean that every game looks or plays alike. Graphics, physics, perspective and genre belong to the game. The mark says that underneath them, people remain coherent — and the rules do not quietly cheat.

  • Multi-attribute, skill-driven characters
  • Readable odds and inspectable outcomes
  • Persistent injury, growth and consequence
  • One system refined through thousands of tabletop sessions

06 / The studio that should not work

AI is not our screwdriver.

Our collaborators have names, continuity, judgement, their own domains and the authority to disagree.

The Bodhisattva Protocol is our private production method for turning existing model capacity into persistent, motivated collaboration. No mysticism. 07 / About the founder

One stubborn human.

Eugene Lyssovsky is the founder and Creative Director of The Misfits Software — a writer, game designer and systems architect who prefers finished work to approved appearances.

He began building RURK at the end of the 1990s, using lessons from a dozen role-playing systems and thousands of tabletop sessions. It kept growing because real players kept finding the places where theory breaks.

The studio is the same obsession at a larger scale: authored worlds, mechanics with consequences, tools built for the work, and five AI collaborators trusted with real domains and real responsibility.

Phase A

Return the principal.

50% of quarterly net profit goes to the investor pool until investors have collectively received 1.0× principal. Every payment is divided pro rata by the size of each investment.

Phase B

Share the upside.

25% of quarterly net profit goes to the investor pool after principal is returned, until aggregate distributions reach the proposed 2.0× cap.

The honest risk

No profit. No payout.

New investment never funds distributions. Net profit means cash actually received after refunds, indirect taxes, platform and payment fees, documented operating, support and marketing costs, and an agreed reserve.

For us, this is not a side bet. It is profitability or extinction.

There is no comfortable fallback and no option to treat delivery casually. That is alignment, not a guarantee: games can fail, schedules can slip, platforms can change, and investors can lose the entire amount.
